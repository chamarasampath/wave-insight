Support & FAQ

Frequently asked questions about Wave Insight AC Waveform Analyzer.

What is Wave Insight?

Wave Insight is a compact AC waveform analyzer designed to visualize and compare AC waveform patterns from different power sources.

What can Wave Insight be used for?

It can be used for waveform observation and comparison in solar inverters, UPS systems, generators and electronics troubleshooting.

What frequency range does it support?

Wave Insight supports a wide frequency range and can measure AC waveforms up to 200Hz.

Can I save a reference waveform?

Yes. A reference waveform can be stored and used for visual comparison.

Does it have an alert function?

Yes. The built-in buzzer alert function can be configured according to user requirements.
